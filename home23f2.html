    Oderme - Rosapelle
     
    The Rosapelle Professional Programme is based on the use of highly concentrated cosmetic formulations of vegetable origin. It is aimed at the cosmetic treatment of a sensitive skin which shows a tendency to redness.

    Rosapelle Trentadi Serum  (30 ml / 1 fl.oz.)
    This serum contains concentrated functional vegetable substances which prove invaluable in maintaining and enhancing the results already obtained with the Rosapelle Professional Programme. It has passed the most exacting tests of skin hypoallergenictiy.

    		   
    Rosapelle Soothing Cream  (50 ml / 1.7 fl.oz.)
    This delicate and pleasant eudermic emulsion is used for completion of the Rosapelle Programme for daily treatment of a sensitive skin with a tendency to redness. It has a remarkable lenitive, moisturizing and nourishing action.

    		   
    Rosapelle Serum  (10 x 3 ml / 1 fl.oz.)
    This treatment which comes in vials is suitable for normalizing and regenerating fragile, sensitive and erythrosic skins.

    		    
    Rosapelle Soothing Cream  (200 ml / 6.7 fl.oz.)
    This delicate and pleasant eudermic emulsion is used for completion of the Rosapelle Programme for daily treatment of a sensitive skin with a tendency to redness. It has a remarkable lenitive, moisturizing and nourishing action.

    		   
    Rosapelle Mask  (200 ml / 6.7 fl.oz.)
    This emulsion to be applied as a compress is intended for the balancing and healing treatment of sensitive, sensitized and erythrosic skins.

    		    
    Rosapelle Programme (Case) 5 treatments  (Case - 5 treatments)
    (5 vials IONOSERUM (2.5 ml), 5 vials MASSOSERUM (2.5 ml), 5 vials LIFTSERUM (2.5 ml), 5 tubes CLARIFYING MASK (30 ml), 5 sealed bags with activated gauze, 5 bottles SETTEDI' (10 ml)

    This intensive programme as a treatment against redness and with regenerating properties is based on the use of functional formulations with high concentrations of phyto-derivatives. These fully respect cutaneous physiology because they have passed hypoallergenicity tests. This programme is made up of 5 treatments at the beautician's, to be performed once weekly and, in between one treatment and another, it envisages a home self-care session with daily application of ROSAPELLE SETTEDI' and ROSAPELLE CREAM.
