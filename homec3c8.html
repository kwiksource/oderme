    Oderme - Penta
     
    Makes a new-concept method based on glycolic acid for the aesthetic treatment of all those imperfections which affect the look of the skin surface: wrinkles, dyschromias, hyperkeratosic and asphyxial areas, impurities, dehydration, photoaging. This method involves a professional treatment to be carried out every weeks for a minimum of 8 treatment (the number of times a week varies in each.

    Cleansing Gel pH 5.2  (200 ml / 6.66 fl.oz.)
    This opalescent gel is based on glycolic acid carried by cyclodextrins, which is particularly suitable for daily cleansing of a skin which is receiving an exfoliating treatment.

    		    
    Lotion Tonic pH 5.8  (250 ml / 8.33 fl.oz.)
    This alcohol-free tonic lotion is ideal as a complement to daily cleansing of a skin under an exfoliating treatment. It prolongs the exfoliating action and imparts firmness to the skin.

    		    
    Lightening Serum pH 5.5  (30 ml / 1 fl.oz.)
    This fluid is successfully used in the treatment of skin dyschromias of different types: chloasms, cicatricial marks, senile stains, hyperpigmentation from overexposure to UV rays. It clarifies pigment stains and improves skin brightness.

    		   
    Nourishing Cream 24 Hour pH 5.1  (50 ml / 1.66 fl.oz.)
    A 24-hour emulsion (day and night) especially suitable for stimulating a progressive process of cell renewal in a reasonable period of time in cases of skins which show the typical anatomic alterations which follow natural senile involution. It has a remarkable renewing affect and makes the skin bright, firm and perfectly rejuvenated.
