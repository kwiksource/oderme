Equipelle Programme

The Equipelle programme based on azelaic acid is a self-contained treatment founded on the most advanced cosmetic research.

Size: Case - 8 treatments 		Benefits:
It is intended to restore balance, well-being and brightness to seborrhoeic, impure skins suffering from acne. In its soluble form, azelaic acid - a very innovative cosmetic substance- in conjunction with other functional substances of vegetable and marine origin, makes the skin transparent, evenly smooth, and fresh, as it fights directly the causes which underlie this skin disorder. Indeed,
  • it restores the upset sebaceous secretion
  • it has an astringent effect on the follicles mitigating the problem of "dilated pores"
  • it encourages the involution of foruncles, comedoes and inflamation ,
  • it moisturizes and provides relief in the of reddish areas ,
  • it refreshes and imparts pleasant tone to the epidermis ,
  • it clarifies pigment spots and scar-marks, making the skin clear and bright.

    The Equipelle Programme is made up of 8 professional sessions to be had every 5-7 days and also includes a highly effective home self-care section which is based on daily use of Settedï¿½ and Equipelle Crema whose formulations are made active thanks to the active compound composed of azelaic acid and an innovative molecule, metacrylic ester, which absorbs excess sebum.

    Ingredients:

    Content:

  • 8 BETA-ALPHA-HYDROXYACIDS 2 ML VIALS
  • 8 MASSOSERUM 3 ml vials
  • 1 PROFESSIONAL MASK 200 ml tube
  • 8 sealed bags with gauze for use as a mask
  • 8 SETTEDI' 10 ml bottles

    MASSOSERUM:
    AZELAIC ACID 6% (sebum-regulating, bacteriostatic, depigmenting)- MUSHROOM EXTRACT 2% (astringent) - CHINCONA EXTRACT 0,1% and TORMENTIL EXTRACT 0,1% (astringent upon the follicle) - FARNESOL 0,5% (antiseptic) - LEMON OIL 0,05 % (antiseptic) - MENTHYL LACTATE 0,6% and WILD MINT OIL 0,05% (refreshing)

    BETA-ALPHA-HYDROXYACIDS:
    GLYCOLIC ACID 8% (exfoliating) - SALICILYC ACID 2% (antiseptic, keratolythic) - AZELAIC ACID 0,1% (depigmenting, bacteriostatic, sebum-regulating)

    PROFESSIONAL MASK:
    MUD 40% (for the absorption of impurities and to fight inflamation) CARRAGEENAN EXTRACT 3% (protective, moisturizing) -LAMINARIA DIGITATA EXTRACT 2% (sebum-regulating, bacteriostatic)- AZELAIC ACID 0,1% (depigmenting, bacteriostatic, sebum-regulating).

    GAUZE:
    AZELAIC ACID 0,5% (sebum-regulating, bacteriostatic, depigmenting)- MUSHROOM EXTRACT 0,5% (astringent) - CHINCONA EXTRACT 0,1% and TORMENTIL EXTRACT 0,1% (astringent upon the follicle and toning) - MENTHYL LACTATE 0,3% and WILD MINT OIL 0,1% (refreshing) - LEMON OIL 0,05% (antiseptic)

    SETTEDI':
    AZELAIC ACID 3% ( sebum-regulating, bacteriostatic, depigmenting) - MUSHROOM EXTRACT 2% (astringent upon dilated pores, opacifying, moisturizing).

    Use: This professional programme is made up of 8 professional sessions to be performed every 5-7 days, and also includes a highly effective home self-care section based on daily use of Settedï¿½ and Equipelle Cream.

    Instructiions for Use: Cleanse the skin thoroughly using EQUIPELLE Cleansing Gel and Spray Tonic. Apply the content of the Equipelle Beta-alpha-hydroxyacids over those areas showing foruncles, comedoes and scar-marks.
    Spread the Equipelle Massoserum vial extensively over face and neck massaging slightly for lympho-drainage.
    Apply the activated gauze included in the sealed bag causing it to perfectly stick to the skin of neck and face. Use a brush to extend over the gauze, a thick layer of Equipelle Professional Mask, leaving it to rest for about 20 minutes.
    Then remove the gauze and remove all traces of the mask with a wet sponge.
    Spray EQUIPELLE Spray Tonicover the skin and complete the treatment using Equipelle Cream.. Give the customer the Equipelle Settedï¿½ and Equipelle Cream s for home treatment.

    Technical Features: Accurate use of this programme helps the beautician to treat the hard problem of acne with guaranteed results.
    The high efficacy of the treatment and of the related home self-care products rests on the following factors:

  • the high concentration of active principles in the formulation;
  • the presence of an active principle, azelaic acid, which has been used in dermatology for a long time and which has now found its place in cosmesis in its water-soluble form,
  • the combination of azelaic acid and a mixture of easily tolerated vegetable substances acting both on sebaceous hypersecretion and skin inflamation. The above features make the Equipelle Programme based on azelaic acid a real "cosmoceutic": i.e., a cosmetic with a real therapeutic action-
