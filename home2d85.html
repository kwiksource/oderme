Equipelle Serum

A balancing treatment suitable for impure skins showing sebaceous hypersecretion, dilated follicles, comedoes, foruncles and thickening of the horny layer.

Size: 10 x 3 ml / 1 fl.oz. 		Benefits:
  • Restores balance to sebaceous secretion reducing all unpleasant aesthetic effects of excess sebum
  • Has an astringent effect on follicles, relieving the problems related to dilated pores
  • Has a cleansing effect on the skin surface
  • Clarifying and soothing action as it mitigates redness and skin spots
  • Refreshes and imparts pleasant tone to the epidermis
    		•
    Ingredients:
    ALFA-BISABOLOLO 0,1% (clarifying, lenitive) - COMPLESSO SEBOEQUILIBRANTE 3% (sebum-balancing) - E.G. CHINA 0,1% and E.G. TORMENTILLA 0,1% (astringent upon the follicles) - FARNESOLO 0,5% (antiseptic) - O.E. LIMONE 0,1% (antiseptic) - LATTATO DI MENTILE 0,5% and O.E. MENTA0,1% (refreshing) - PANTENOLO 1% (moisturizing, protective)- ACIDO AZELAICO (sebum-regulating, bacteriostatic, depigmenting).

    Use: This product is intended for use in the beautician's booth as part of a professional treatment for impure, seborrhoeic and acneic skins. Indeed, the METODICA PROFESSIONALE EQUIPELLE includes the application of EQUIPELLE SERUM followed by EQUIPELLE MASCHERA CREMA.

    Instructions for Use: After cleansing the skin of face and neck with EQUIPELLE GEL DI PULIZIA and TONICO SPRAY, apply the whole content of the EQUIPELLE SERUM vial extensively over the skin and massage slightly until the product has been completely absorbed. Then pass on to apply EQUIPELLE MASCHERA CREMA following the appropriate method.

    Technical Features: The antiseptic and decongestioning effects may be enhanced by applying high frequency.
