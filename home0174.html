Fitopack Algae Marine (Face & Body)

This powder is entirely made up of micronized algae extracts of the Fucus Vesiculous type. Ideal product for a classic treatment of wellness and relax.

Size: 2 x 200 gm / 7 oz 		Benefits:
This product is activated by the addition of a specific product known as "Hammamelis (Witch hazel) Activator", with which it forms a creamy mixture of easy application over all the body.It is this combination which produces beneficial effects of its components.Indeed, the Bladder Wrack Alga (Fucus Vesiculous) is a concentration of various active principles which prove highly functional from the cosmetic point of view containing as much as 25-30% of minerals. Its high mineral content includes a number of trace elements, among which: iodine (0.05%), iron (from 0.05% to 0.15%), manganese (0.009%), copper (0.03%) and traces of cobalt, zinc, nickel, barium, boron, chromium, lithium, silicon and so on.On the cosmetic level, Fitopack Alghe Marine provides invaluable help in the treatment of edematous and fibrous cellulitis, of localized adiposity and in the case of an anelastic tissue contaminated by toxins and with signs of ageing. When in contact with the micronized Bladder Wrack (Fucus Vesiculous) Alga, the skin undergoes important biochemical processes:
  • Disintoxication
  • Remineralization
  • Stimulation of the metabolism of adipocytes
  • Improvement of peripheral microcirculation
  • Pleasant sensation of wellness and relax
    • Ingredients:
    Micronized FUCUS VESICULOUS 100% (lipolytic, remineralizing, toning, detoxifying)

    Application: This professional product is especially suitable for application in the beautician's booth to treat a tissue affected by edematous, fibrous cellulitis or by a mixed form (cellulitis and localized adiposity) or showing signs of skin ageing. The treatment is preceded by a preparatory stage consisting of the application of other specific products (SINERGIE ESSENZIALI and OLIGOELEMENTI) belonging to the SYNERDERME following the instructions in the method used.

    Instructions For Use: Pour the whole content of a one-dose pack (100g) into a bowl and mix the powder with about 200 ml of "Hammamelis Activator" until it becomes a soft homogeneous mixture.Apply the solution obtained with the help of a brush over all areas to be treated so as to cover the skin surface. Then wrap a sheet of cartene around and leave to rest for about 20 minutes.Then proceed with hydromassage using the specific product of the SYNERDERME line, i.e.,HYDROBENDE. Finish up the treatment with a short massage using Profilderme Crema Alghe.

    Technical Features: The product appears in the form of powder, so that it represents an alternative to other poultices in the Thalassoderme Line of ready-to-use muds.Besides, it constitutes a professional universal sort of treatment which may be given only for toning, remineralizing purposes on the whole body, including the scalp.Intended as a nutritional integrator, this product may also be taken orally in the morning (1 tea-spoonful in half a glass of water) on an empty stomach to support the external aesthetic treatment.
