Lightening Serum pH 5.5

This fluid is successfully used in the treatment of skin dyschromias of different types: chloasms, cicatricial marks, senile stains, hyperpigmentation from overexposure to UV rays. It clarifies pigment stains and improves skin brightness.

Size: 30 ml / 1 fl.oz. 		Benefits:
The depigmenting effect on the stains is caused by an innovative combination of functional substances of natural origin, which have a long-lasting and effective clarifying effect.
  • Gently exfoliates the horny lamellae on the surface thanks to the action exercised by the alphahydroxyacids, and ecourages the elimination of pigment build-up.
  • Gradually clarifies pigment stains because Kojic acid
  • Guarantees secure and long.lasting results
    		•
    Ingredients:
    ALPHAHYDROXYACIDS from COCONUT, BANANA, LEMON and CUCUMBER EXTRACT 4% (exfoliating effect)

    Application: This product is for use at home with the PENTA method for clarifying pigment stains. Successful results are guaranteed after at least two months of daily application. During the two-month treatment, it is advisable to apply the product twice (in the morning and evening) while one evening application is enough during the maintenance period.

    Instructions For Use: After cleansing the skin with cleansing gel and tonic lotion, apply a small amount of lightening gel by gently massaging the areas to be treated.

    Technical Features: Kogjic acid was introduced in the formulation for depigmentation purposes. This is a natural molecule extracted from rice and shows no risk of undesirable side-effects. It offers an alternative solution to the use of hydroquinone - a synthetic molecule- which may give rise to toxicity if excessively used and which is bound to be banned from cosmetics.
