Rosapelle Programme (Case) 5 treatments

(5 vials IONOSERUM (2.5 ml), 5 vials MASSOSERUM (2.5 ml), 5 vials LIFTSERUM (2.5 ml), 5 tubes CLARIFYING MASK (30 ml), 5 sealed bags with activated gauze, 5 bottles SETTEDI' (10 ml)

This intensive programme as a treatment against redness and with regenerating properties is based on the use of functional formulations with high concentrations of phyto-derivatives. These fully respect cutaneous physiology because they have passed hypoallergenicity tests. This programme is made up of 5 treatments at the beautician's, to be performed once weekly and, in between one treatment and another, it envisages a home self-care session with daily application of ROSAPELLE SETTEDI' and ROSAPELLE CREAM.

Size: Case - 5 treatments

Benefits:
  • It mitigates the irritation often found in particularly sensitive and reddish skins
  • It has a remarkable regenerating action in the case of a skin with a natural tendency to exposure to environmental factors. It regulates and reinstates the epidermic hydro-lipidic
layer, yielding important moisturizing factors and skin-compatible lipids which prove so important in ensuring the integrity and protection of the skin surface.

Use: Cleanse the skin using PREDERME UNIVERSAL CLEANSING MILK and SPRAY TONIC Spread by dabbing lightly a few drops of Synerderme Sinergia Ess. CM and Oligo CM over reddened areas. Apply the same products on the solar plexus and the temples with decontracting movements. Apply a few drops of Synerderme Sinergia Ess. DR and Oligo DR over the main lymph-node clusters and perform lympho-draining massage. Spread the ROSAPELLE JONOSERUM vial over the face, neck and décolleté and encourage penetration by "Gioielli del viso" machine. Using the ROSAPELLE MASSOSERUM vial, perform a draining massage. Spread the activated rectangular gauze - this comes in a sealed bag - over the face and neck making sure it adheres perfectly. With the help of a brush, apply a thick layer of ROSAPELLE CLARIFYING MASK on the gauze over the skin of face and neck and leave to rest for 20 minutes. Once resting time is over remove the gauze and the mask and cleanse any residue off by using damp sponges. Complete treatment by dabbing ROSAPELLE LIFTSERUM vial very lightly.
Usage:
  1. Cleanse the skin using PREDERME UNIVERSAL CLEANSING MILK and UNIVERSAL SPRAY TONIC.
  2. Spread the content of the ROSAPELLE JONOSERUM vial over the skin of face, neck and décolleté and encourage penetration by "Gioielli del viso" machine.
  3. Massage using the ROSAPELLE MASSOSERUM vial.
  4. Spread the rectangular activated gauze which comes in a sealed bag over the skin of face and neck making it adhere closely to the skin.
  5. With the help of a brush, apply on the gauze on the face and neck a thick layer of ROSAPELLE CLARIFYING MASK and leave it to rest for about 20 minutes.
  6. Once resting time has passed, remove the gauze and the mask and cleanse any rests of the product with damp sponges.
  7. Complete the treatment using ROSAPELLE LIFTSERUM by massaging lightly and delicately.
  8. Give the customer the bottle containing SETTEDI' and LIFTPELLE CREAM for home self-care.
