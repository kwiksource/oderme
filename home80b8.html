Liftpelle Programme

(5 JONOSERUM vials (2.5 ml each), 5 MASSOSERUM vials (2.5 ml each), 5 LIFTSERUM vials (2.5 ml each), 5 BIOACTIVE MASK 30 ml tubes, 5 SETTEDI' 10 ml bottles, 5 sealed bags containing ACTIVATED GAUZE)

The intensive professional LIFTPELLE programme has a high concentration of functional substances which characterize its formulation. Thanks to these substances, the programme exercises a reinforced action against wrinkles and against the alterations which naturally follow the involutive process of tissue and which become more and more noticeable as time passes. The programme is composed of 5 professional treatments to be performed at the beautician's. These are to be done on a weekly basis and, in between one treatment and another, LIFTPELLE SETTEDI' and LIFTPELLE CREAM must be applied daily.

Size: Case - 5 treatments

Benefits:
  • Creates a biological ambient which encourages optimal activity of the skin tissue
  • Enhances tome and compactness in the epidermis
  • Produces just the right tension to improve skin trophism, with noticeable reduction of the most superficial wrinkles.
  • Optimalizes the water-lipid ratio in the horny layer.

    Intended Use: This develops through 5 professional treatments in the beautician's booth, to be performed on a weekly basis. The LIFTPELLE PROGRAMME , besides, envisages home daily application of LIFTPELLE SETTEDI' and LIFTPELLE CREAM in between one treatment and another.

    Technical Features: This particular method stems from the combination of the most advanced research in the field of "Functional Cosmesis" and the personal experience of the beautician. The result of this important interaction allowed the formulation of the LIFTPELLE PROGRAMME, which aims at tackling the problem of skin ageing, both with a nourishing And stimulating action and with a relaxing action exercised on the creases and wrinkles. After the treatment has been completed, the results is, precisely, comparable to that of "lifting".

    Some products have been changed : LIFTSERUM vial : new formula and new texture; THE ACTIVED GAUZE : new formula and new texture (microemulsion); SETTEDI' : new formula and new texture.

    Usage:
    1. Before starting the treatment is important to prepare the skin with Sinergie Essenziali and Oligo of Synerderme line.
    2. Cleanse the skin using PREDERME UNIVERSAL CLEANSING MILK and TONING LOTION
    3. Spread a vial of LIFTPELLE JONOSERUM over the skin of face, neck and décolleté and encourage penetration by means of ionophoresis.
    4. Massage using the LIFTPELLE MASSOSERUM vial
    5. With the help of a brush, apply LIFTPELLE BIOACTIVE MASK over the surface of face and neck in the direction of the muscular fibres.
    6. Apply in the same way the activated gauze contained in a sealed bag and exercise a "pulling" action.
    7. Once resting time (20 minutes) has passed, remove the gauze and the mask.
    8. Use the LIFTPELLE LIFTSERUM vial and massage with upward "pulling" movements to complete the treatment.
    9. Give the customer the bottle containing LIFTPELLE SETTEDI' and LIFTPELLE CREAM for home self-care.
