Equipelle Purifying Cream Mask

This mask in the form of an emulsion is intended for an intensive treatment of a seborrhoeic, impure skin with a marked tendency to acne.

Size: 200 ml / 6.7 fl.oz. 		Benefits:
  • Has a marked absorbing effect on sebaceous impurities.
  • Balances sebaceous secretion mitigating all related disorders It maintains an optimal water rate on the epicutaneous water-lypidic mantel
  • Has a soothing effect on widespread redness which are often found in impure skins while also imparting a pleasant sensation of freshness.
    		•
    Ingredients:
    ALFA BISABOLOLO 0,5% (clarifying, lenitive) - ALOE VERA GEL 50% (moisturizing, soothing, protective) - CAOLINO 10% (lightening, lenitive) - ALLANTOINA 0,5% (emolient, keratolythic) - CARBOSSIMETILCISTEINATO DI LISINA 0,5% - (sebum-balancing) - COMPLESSO SEBOEQUILIBRANTE 0,5% (sebum-balancing) - LATTATO DI MENTILE 0,8% and O.E MENTA 0,1% (refreshing) - O.E. LIMONE 0,2% (antiseptic) - VIT. E ACETATO 0,5% (eutrophic, it fights redness, antioxidant and, consequently, anti-free radicals)- ACIDO AZELAICO (sebum-regulating, bacteriostatic, depigmenting).

    Use: This product is intended for professional use in the beautician's booth to treat an impure, seborrhoeic, acneic skin. The EQUIPELLE PROFESSIONAL METHOD actually includes preventive application of EQUIPELLE SERUM and then of EQUIPELLE PURIFYING CREAM MASK. For home self-care we also recommend a weekly application of EQUIPELLE PURIFYING CREAM MASK.

    Instructions for Use: Once the skin of face and neck has been cleansed with EQUIPELLE CLEANSING GEL and SPRAY TONIC the EQUIPELLE SERUM vial and allow penetration by massaging slightly. Later, apply a gauze imbibed with EQUIPELLE SPRAY TONIC on face and neck and, with the help of a brush, apply a thick layer of EQUIPELLE PURIFYING MASK over it.
    Once the 20 minutes allowed for resting time are over, take the gauze off and use wet sponges to remove any residue left after application.

    Technical Features The particular formulation of this product gave as a result a compress emulsion of particular softness. It exercises an absorbing action without undergoing any drying during resting time, which makes mask removal easier.
