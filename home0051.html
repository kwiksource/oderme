Liftpelle Bioactive Mask

This emulsion to be applied as a compress, is suitable for an "anti-age" treatment in the case of mature and devitalized skins with the typical anatomic alterations brought about by the natural process of senile involution. It reinstates elasticity and tone in the epidermic tissue, mitigating wrinkling and producing a toning effect on the skin. It improves the appearance of the complexion by reducing pigment marks, which are characteristic of mature skins.

Size: 200 ml / 6.7 fl.oz. 		Benefits:
  • Helps fight the cutaneous oxidising processes caused by free radicals, which speed up the ageing process.
  • Reinstates the hydro-lipid balance on the epidermic surface.
    		•
    Ingredients:
    AOSAINE 2% (derived from Ulva lactuca, it stimulates energy metabolism of the cutaneous tissue by encouraging collagen synthesis) - COMPOUND BASED ON CARTEGEENAN EXTRACT 2.5% (this alga has remarkable moisturizing and protective properties) - ASCORBIL METHYL SILOXANE PECTINATE 1% (this compound is composed of Ascorbic acid and Silicon, which is indispensable in activating the collagen synthesis in the dermis) - COLA EXTRACT 1% - GINSENG EXTRACT 2% - GUARANA' 1% (this stimulates cell metabolism) - CONE FLOWER EXTRACT 1% (a stimulant of collagen synthesis in the dermis)

    Intended Use: This product is intended for use in the beautician's cabin as part of a professional treatment aimed at wrinkled and alipic skins. The PROFESSIONAL LIFTPELLE method actually includes preventive application of LIFTPELLE SERUM and of LIFTPELLE BIOACTIVE MASK to follow.

    Technical Features: LIFTPELLE BIOACTIVE MASK is used as part of the PROFESSIONAL LIFTPELLE PROGRAMME. During the treatment, the elasticizing and relaxing effect produced on the skin folds which are peculiar to the mask, is enhanced by the particular method of application on the mask of the "activated gauze" included in the PROGRAMME. The "pulling effect", indeed, produces a truly relaxing effect on the features.

    Usage:
    Apply the entire content of a LIFTPELLE SERUM vial on the skin of face, neck and décolleté once proper cleansing and toning have been completed using PREDERME UNIVERSAL CLEANSING MILK and SPRAY TONIC. After encouraging penetration of the product by means of slight massage, use a brush to spread a thick layer of LIFTPELLE BIOACTIVE MASK over the face, neck and décolleté in the direction of the muscular fibres. Once the 20-minute resting time has passed, remove the mask using a damp sponge and complete the treatment using LIFTINGDERME CREAM OVER 35 or LIFTPELLE CREAM.
