Rosapelle Soothing Cream

This delicate and pleasant eudermic emulsion is used for completion of the Rosapelle Programme for daily treatment of a sensitive skin with a tendency to redness. It has a remarkable lenitive, moisturizing and nourishing action.

Size: 50 ml / 1.7 fl.oz. 		Benefits:
This emulsion of remarkable decongesting and healing properties is intended for the treatment of sensitive, erythrosic skin.
  • It has a remarkable healing action on skins with a marked tendency to irritation
  • It regulates and restores the epidermic hydro-lipidic layer yielding important functional factors which ensure the integrity and protection of the skin surface.
  • Its particular UVA and UVB filters protect the skin from any damage caused by ultraviolet radiation.
    		•
    Ingredients:
    VEGETABLE KERAMIDS 2% (they reduce trans-epidermic water loss and therefore have a protective and moisturizing action) - PANTHENOL 2% (moisturizing, protective and lenitive) - AESCULIN 1% (for the prevention of edemas and vessel protection) - BILLBERRY LIPOSOME 1% (for vessel protection) - ROSE MUSK OIL 1% (to reduce trans-epidermic water loss and to improve the composition of the epicutaneous lipid segment, exercising a moisturizing protective action) - Vit. E 0.5% (with eutrophic properties and for fighting free radicals and, therefore, wrinkles) -GLYCYRRHETINIC ACID 0.5% (re-epithelizing, healing) - UVA and UVB filters 0.3% (for protection against UV radiations)

    Application: This is an excellent product for home self-care, both by day and by night, to be used as part of the ROSAPELLE PROGRAMME. It is also particularly suitable for completion of the professional treatment in the beautician's booth while the ROSAPELLE PROGRAMME is in progress.

    Instructions For Use: Once the skin of face and neck has been perfectly cleansed and toned with Prederme UNIVERSAL CLEANSING MILK and UNIVERSAL TONING LOTION, apply ROSAPELLE CREAM massaging delicately until the product is completely absorbed.

    Technical Features: The particular functional substances in its formulation and the degree of their concentration place this product on top of the line for efficacy and quality.
