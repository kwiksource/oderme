Cleansing Gel pH 5.2

This opalescent gel is based on glycolic acid carried by cyclodextrins, which is particularly suitable for daily cleansing of a skin which is receiving an exfoliating treatment.

Size: 200 ml / 6.66 fl.oz. 		Benefits:
On account of the particular balance among the cleansing factor and the functional substances in it, this gel:
  • encourages a gradual and delicate exfoliating action even at the cleansing stage thanks to the glycolic acid�cyclodextrin complex.
  • keeps the moisture rate of the epidermis at its best and prevents all possible irritation because of the soluble Collagen and Panthenol it contains.
  • perfectly cleanses the skin with delicate surface-active agents which ensure the successful removal of any impurity.
    • Ingredients:
    GLYCOLIC ACID IN CYCLODEXTRIN 1% (a complex made up of a carrying system - cyclodextrins and glycolic acid - with an exfoliating action), HYDROLYZED COLLAGEN 0.2% (substantivating, conditioning, moisturizing), D-PANTHENOL 0.2% (moisturizing, lenitive).

    Application: This product is both for use at home, for ordinary evening cleansing and for professional treatment. It helps to remove make-up and skin impurities before starting apply PENTA Product.

    Instructions For Use: Apply a slight layer of cleansing gel over the area to be cleansed. Emulsify with water until slightly foamy. Rinse thoroughly and tone with TONIC LOTION.

    Technical Features: This cleansing product is based on an oil-free formulation, which may be perfectly rinsed with water.
    As a result, it leaves a dry, soft, perfectly cleansed skin with the right degree of moisture. These characteristics make it ideal for use even without exfoliating treatment. It is particularly suitable for seborrhoeic, thickened and hyperkeratotic skin or in the case of those end customers who wish to try a different way of cleansing the skin other than the traditional cleansing milk.
