Hydrapelle Programme

This articulated method has been spedifically studied to tackle the problem of skin dehydration. The combined action of 5 complementary formulations is carried out on different levels of the skin tissue.

Size: Case - 5 treatments 		Benefits:
  • Has an anti-evaporation effect, thanks to the presence of vegetable keramids, which prove indispensable in maintaining the right degree of epidermic moisture. Indeed, since these strentghen the "cellular cement", they limit the amount of outgoing evaporated water as well as the water-repellency of the outermost part of the horny layer.
  • Normalizes the water content in the epidermis with a positive influence on the cutaneous physiology, thus improving its appearance. Collagen, hyaluronic acid and aloe vera gel create the phenomena connected with precocious senile involution.
  • Slows down the ageing process thanks to the presence of tocopherol (vit.E), whose anti-oxidant action combats the oxidating reactions caused by free radicals on the biological membranes and structural proteins.
  • Prevents on the skin surface the damages caused by pollution, increases superficial hydration of the whole epidermis and produces a nourishing effect thanks to the presence of Omegaplankton.

    Content:
  • 5 JONOSERUM 2.5 ml vials

  • 5 SETTEDI' 10 ml bottles

    Main Functional Substances:

  • JONOSERUM Natural Moisturizing Factor 5% ( moisturizing ), Panthenol 2%(protective, moisturizing, soothing ), Urea 2% (emollient).
  • MASSOSERUM Vit.E (anti-age, free -radical scavenger ), Jojoba oil 1% and Shea butter 1 % (emollient, nourishing), Vegetal keramids 1%( moisturizing), Omegaplankton 0,5% (moisturizing, nourishing, anti-pollution)
  • VELLOSERUM Aloe vera gel 20% (moisturizing, soothing, protective), Menthyl lactate 0,8% ( refreshing), Allantoin 0,4% ( emollient).
  • LIFTSERUM Fagus sylvatica extract 2% (contents "phytostimulines" anti-age, repairing, hydrating).
  • SETTEDI' Panthenol 2%(protective, moisturizing, soothing ), Sodium hyaluronate 0,5% (hydrating ), Omegaplankton 0,5% ( moisturizing, nourishing, anti-pollution).
  • COLLAGEN MASK Soluble collagen 100% ( hydrating).

    Application : Its application is made up of 5 professional treatments at the beautician's, to be carried out once weekly. Home self-care is also necessary in between sessions with the use of Hydrapelle Setted� and Hydrapelle Cream.

    Usage:
    1. Cleanse the skin with Prederme Universal Cleansing Milk and Spray Tonic.
    2. Spread the Jonoserum vial over the face, the neck and area surrounding the neck and help penetrate by means os "Gioielli del viso" equipment.
    3. Massage using Massoserum vial.
    4. Imbibe the Collagen Mask with the content of Velloserum bottle and apply it on face and neck, making it adhere to the skin surface perfectly and leaving it to rest for 25-30 minutes.
    5. Using the Liftserum vial perform an upward traction massage to complete the treatment.
